News

This section contains the Olb City Announcer, which is a weekly newspaper that provides comprehensive news from around Arenztopia. The articles are copied exactly as they were in print, with the exception of advertisements, pictures, and comics. Symbols that were not able to be reproduced are described (ex. degree symbol in newspaper = the word degrees in digital reproduction)
